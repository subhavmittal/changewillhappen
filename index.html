Become a Changemaker

Our health goes beyond the biological: it is social, environmental, and deeply personal. It is shaped by what we do, think, feel, and the spaces we move through every day. Change Will Happen advances healthcare through initiatives centered on environmental action, preventative interventions, and community-based care. By improving access to services and empowering people to advocate for themselves, we are creating the conditions for lasting, collective well-being.

COVID Relief

COVID Relief

During the COVID-19 pandemic, we produced and distributed N95 masks to healthcare workers and communities in need. This initiative helped protect frontline workers and vulnerable populations.

The Battery Project

The Battery Project

We collect partially used batteries from medical offices and redistribute them for free across New York City. Since 2016, we have reused over 28,000 batteries, preventing hazardous waste and protecting our environment.

Medical Missions

Medical Missions

Since 2018, we have organized 4 medical missions across the Philippines and Haiti, serving over 1,000 patients and bringing essential healthcare to communities in need.

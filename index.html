Change Will Happen
Making the world a healthier place, one step at a time

Our health goes beyond the biological: it is social, environmental, and deeply personal. It is shaped by what we do, think, feel, and the spaces we move through every day. Change Will Happen advances healthcare through initiatives centered on environmental action, preventative interventions, and community-based care. By improving access to services and empowering people to advocate for themselves, we are creating the conditions for lasting, collective well-being.

COVID Relief

COVID Relief

During the COVID-19 pandemic, we produced and distributed N95 masks to healthcare workers and communities in need. This initiative helped protect frontline workers and vulnerable populations during the crisis

The Battery Project

The Battery Project

We collect partially used batteries from medical offices and redistribute them for free across New York City. Since 2016, we have reused over 24,000 batteries and distributed 800+ packets, preventing hazardous waste and protecting our environment.

Learn More

Medical Mission to Philippines

Medical Mission to Philippines

We launched a medical mission to support the Mangyan communities on Mindoro after disasters threatened their health. Despite volcano eruptions and COVID-19, we raised over $16,000 and delivered essential healthcare to remote villages.

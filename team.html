About

Avni Garg is a senior at Stuyvesant High School, a junior docent at the Staten Island Zoo, and the President of Change Will Happen Inc. Inspired by her first international medical mission to Haiti in 2018, she has since been committed to service-driven initiatives that uplift communities. Passionate about science, nature, and education, Avni strives to engage young minds while advocating for environmental preservation. A classical music performer and dance enthusiast, she believes in nurturing both a healthy mind and body, and aspires to bridge science and music to make learning accessible to all

Your Donation Creates Real Change

Change Will Happen is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization committed to making healthcare accessible and protecting our environment. Your tax-deductible contribution directly funds critical programs that serve underserved communities and promote environmental sustainability.

How Your Gift Helps

$25

Provides basic medical supplies for one family in need

$50

Funds collection and distribution of 100 reusable batteries

$100

Supports health screenings and consultations for multiple families

$250+

Enables a complete medical mission or environmental program
